## 📌 Project Overview

Retail companies generate massive amounts of customer transaction data every day. This project analyzes shopping behavior to uncover patterns in customer spending, loyalty, subscriptions, discounts, product performance, and purchasing trends.

The project follows a complete analytics lifecycle:

- 📥 Data Cleaning using Python
- 🗄️ SQL Analysis using PostgreSQL
- 📊 Interactive Dashboard in Power BI
- 📑 Business Report with Strategic Recommendations

---

# 🎯 Business Problem

The company wanted to answer:

> **How can customer shopping data be leveraged to improve revenue, customer engagement, and long-term loyalty?**

The objective was to identify high-value customers, optimize discounts, improve subscription conversion, and support business decision-making through interactive analytics.

# 🔍 Business Insights

### 👨 Revenue by Gender

- Male customers generated nearly **2×** the revenue compared to female customers.

---

### 💸 High Spending Discount Users

- **839 customers** spent above average despite using discounts.

---

### ⭐ Top Rated Products

- Gloves
- Sandals
- Boots
- Hats
- Skirts

---

### 📦 Subscription Analysis

- Only **27%** customers are subscribed.
- Significant opportunity to increase recurring revenue.

---

### 🎯 Customer Segmentation

- Loyal Customers : **3,116**
- Returning Customers : **701**
- New Customers : **83**

---

### 🚚 Shipping Analysis

Express shipping customers spend slightly more than Standard shipping customers.
